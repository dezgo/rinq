3 Verify Registration

After the phone reboots, check that it registered successfully:

  • On the phone screen: Settings > Status > Lines > Line 1 should show Registered
  • Try making a test call to confirm outbound works
  • Have someone call you to confirm inbound works

Troubleshooting

Line shows "Unregistered" Check username and password match your SIP credentials. Verify Expires is set to 600 or higher.
Outbound works, inbound doesn't Check the Authentication Domain doesn't have a port suffix (e.g. :5060). This is the most common cause.
Audio cuts out or one-way audio Try disabling SIP ALG on your office router. Check that UDP ports 10000-20000 are not blocked.
423 "Interval too brief" Expires must be at least 600. Twilio rejects shorter registration intervals.
High latency / poor audio Ensure the Outbound Proxy and Line 1 Server use the Sydney edge (sip.sydney.twilio.com). Without it, traffic routes through the US.
