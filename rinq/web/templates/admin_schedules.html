📋 Closures (holidays, weekly exceptions, etc.)
How closures work: Closures override business hours. When a call comes in, the system checks closures before business hours — if a closure matches, the caller hears that closure's message instead of the normal routing.

Priority order: If multiple closures match the same day (e.g. a Saturday that falls during Christmas), the most specific one wins: specific dates (e.g. 25 Dec 2026) take priority over recurring annual dates (e.g. every 25 Dec), which take priority over weekly closures (e.g. every Saturday).

Each closure can have its own action and audio message. If not set, it falls back to the schedule's closure defaults above, then to the call flow's closed settings.
